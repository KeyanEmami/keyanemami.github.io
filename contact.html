COMMISSIONING

I enthusiastically welcome commissions from individual musicians, ensembles, concert presenters, music producers, and music supervisors for a diverse range of projects – whether it's your next concert, film, animation, or game. Explore the possibilities and let's create something extraordinary together. For more details on rates and options, please refer to the Canadian League of Composers’ commissioning rates. Want to optimize costs? Consider joint-commissioning with other musicians or ensembles to share the experience and expenses while still achieving a remarkable outcome.'

PRIVATE MUSIC INSTRUCTION & RCM EXAMINER

I offer exclusive private music lessons both in-person at my professional music studio and through video conferencing. Additionally, I serve as an examiner at the Royal Conservatory College of Examiners.
