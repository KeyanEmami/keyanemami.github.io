Dr. Keyan Emami is a JUNO-nominated composer, pianist, and dedicated music educator with over 25 years of experience. He specializes in teaching piano, theory, harmony, solfège, and composition to students of all ages and levels.

As a member of the Royal Conservatory of Music College of Examiners and an RCM Certified Music Teacher, Dr. Emami actively participates in professional associations like the Ontario Registered Music Teachers’ Association and the Canadian Federation of Music Teachers’ Association.

In addition to his private teaching practice, Dr. Emami has instructed courses in piano, music theory, sight singing, ear training, history, harmony, counterpoint, and composition at esteemed institutions, including the University of Toronto, Tehran University of Art, Azad University, and Tehran Conservatory of Music. He employs a diverse range of resources, guiding his students towards realizing their full musical potential through a step-by-step approach.

Renowned for his contemporary extended tonal language and innovative sonic textures, Dr. Emami's award-winning compositions draw inspiration from Persian music and art heritage. His music, performed by leading orchestras, ensembles, and musicians globally, has been featured at prestigious venues such as Four Seasons Centre, Toronto Centre for the Arts, Aga Khan Museum, and Roy Thompson Hall.

Recipient of multiple grants, including the Canada Council for the Arts Grant, Ontario Arts Council Grant, and Toronto Arts Council Grant, Dr. Emami is also an Associate Composer at the Canadian Music Centre, and a member of the Canadian League of Composers and SOCAN.

Dr. Emami completed his doctorate in music at the University of Toronto in 2020, following a B.Eng. in Polymer Engineering and an M.Mus. in Music Composition with first-degree honors. Notably, he was awarded the Best Master’s Thesis Prize for his Symphony in Three Movements. His major studies include mentorship under Christos Hatzis, Alexander Rapoport, and Norbert Palej.

As the founder and academic manager of Key Music, Dr. Emami currently teaches piano, composition, and theory at Key Music, a professional studio in Newmarket. He offers both in-person and online lessons.
