Welcome

Welcome to my website, where we journey into the depths of sound. Inspired by the infinite nuances of music, my compositions are explorations of emotion and expression. From the haunting melodies of 'Kian in Rainbows' to the introspective musings of 'The Black Fish,' each piece invites you to lose yourself in the beauty and mystery of sonic expression. Join me in navigating the vast expanse of musical possibility, uncovering hidden and eternal truths within.

What Critics Are Saying

"In his three-movement inspirational work, Keyan Emami has crafted a multi-stylistic masterpiece that showcases his masterful ability to seamlessly blend elements from the symphonic, jazz, rock, world, and contemporary music genres."

— Tiina Kiik, THE WHOLE NOTE MAGAZINE, on The Black Fish


"Keyan Emami's 'Kian in Rainbows' gleams with color and texture, interspersing musical ideas effectively with text. The lush sounds combine with a dark edge suited to the tragic event that this work memorializes."

— Helen Pridmore, MUSICWORKS MAGAZINE, on Kian in Rainbows


"Keyan Emami's Kian in Rainbows eloquently and poignantly captures the fleeting beauty of childhood amidst darkness, with haunting melodies and poignant expression. A remarkable piece."

— Victor Lazarov, LA SCENA MUSICALE, on Kian in Rainbows


“Keyan Emami's expressive language in '5 Pieces for Piano,' particularly evident in 'In Search of Elusive Memories,' 'Desperate in Darkness,' and 'Ecstatic,' explores diverse dimensions of crafting compositions inspired by Persian motifs and rhythms...This innovative interpretation of Persian piano music redefines conventional norms, offering a fresh perspective on the genre.”

— Arvin Sedaghatkish, Noise Reviews, on 5 Pieces for Piano

Featured Works:

“The Black Fish”

  • Acknowledgement: Recipient of JUNO Award nomination for Classical Composition of the Year
  • Description: A 40-minute journey of introspective inspiration, weaving repetition, improvisation, and diverse genres, echoing the essence of Terry Riley's 'In C' and the spirit of Samad Behrangi’s The Little Black Fish.

“Keyan in Rainbows”

  • Acknowledgement: Received critical acclaim

“Green: 5 Pieces for Piano”
